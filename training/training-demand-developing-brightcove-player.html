Training on Demand: Developing with the Brightcove Player

Viewing the Developing with the Brightcove Player training course is a great way to become familiar with developing with the Brightcove Player.

This course was designed for developers who have experience working with HTML, JavaScript and CSS. It is also helpful to have some working experience with Video Cloud. For an overview of working with Video Cloud and Video Cloud Studio, watch Training on Demand: Introduction to Video Cloud.
