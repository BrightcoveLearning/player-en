What is Brightcove Player debug mode?

When used, player debug mode causes player and plugins to show parts of their logs in the console to help in troubleshooting issues. Currently not every piece of the player interacts with debug mode, but more will in the future.

There are a number of ways to "turn it off and on" and also control behavior, detailed later in this document.
