Introduction

Google provides Interactive Media Ads (IMA) as a suite of SDKs that make it easy to integrate multimedia ads into your websites and apps. There are two ways to implement IMA SDKs:

  • Client-side: this combines ad and content video within your app, and dynamic ad insertion (DAI), which combines ad and content video on the Ad Manager servers, and then returns a single video stream to your app for playback.
  • Dynamic ad insertion (DAI): incorporates ad and content video on the Ad Manager servers and then returns a single video stream to your app for playback.
