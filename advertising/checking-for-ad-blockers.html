Using the usingAdBlocker() method

Brightcove Player has as method that that checks to see if an ad blocker is being used by the browser player your video. This method is usingAdBlocker(). Once the determination is made if an ad blocker is present, your business rules will guide you on further actions. In the following example three ads will be displayed during the video, or if an ad blocker is present a message is displayed below the player.
