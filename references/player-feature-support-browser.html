Note: As of July 1, 2018, the Brightcove Player will no longer support all versions of Microsoft Internet Explorer prior to version 11, including IE 8, 9, and 10. Versions of the Brightcove Player released before July 1 may continue to work on IE 10 and below, but Brightcove will no longer test on any of those browsers or consider bug fixes specific to those versions.
